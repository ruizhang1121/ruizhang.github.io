Research Interests

My research interests are broadly in next-generation communication systems and networks. Currently, I'm particularly interested in the following topics:

  • Fiber-wireless integrated system design

  • Statistical signal processing, waveform design and architecture optimization for high-capacity communication

  • Photonics device and noise modeling for optical communications

  • Joint communication and sensing
